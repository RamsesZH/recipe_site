Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!"




Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy10min25min35min

Ingredients

Black chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the Flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
